Chicken Rice

Chicken rice is a popular Singaporean dish featuring tender poached or roasted chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken broth. It’s typically accompanied by chili sauce, ginger paste, and a side of soup, making it a simple yet flavorful comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (whole or chicken parts like thighs or breasts)
  • Jasmine rice
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Chili sauce
  • Ginger paste
  • Soup (optional, made with chicken broth)

Steps

  1. Rinse the jasmine rice thoroughly, then cook it in chicken broth for extra flavor.
  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add the chicken. Poach the chicken for about 30-40 minutes until fully cooked, or roast it in the oven if preferred.
  3. While the chicken cooks, heat some vegetable oil in a pan and sauté the garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the pan with the sautéed garlic and ginger. Drizzle sesame oil over the rice and mix well to coat.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pot, rub with a little sesame oil, and season with salt. Then, slice the chicken into pieces.
  6. Serve the sliced chicken over the rice and accompany it with chili sauce and ginger paste on the side.
  7. Optionally, serve with a small bowl of the chicken broth as a soup on the side.
