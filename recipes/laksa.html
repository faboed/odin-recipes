Laksa is a rich, spicy noodle soup from Singapore that blends creamy coconut milk with fragrant spices and chili. It typically features rice noodles, shrimp, fish cake, and herbs, creating a bold and comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Coconut milk
  • Laksa paste (chili, spices, shrimp paste)
  • Shrimp
  • Fish cake
  • Bean sprouts
  • Fresh herbs (such as laksa leaves or cilantro)

Steps

  1. Boil the rice noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat some oil and saut√© the laksa paste until fragrant.
  3. Add the coconut milk to the pot and bring to a simmer.
  4. Once the soup base is simmering, add the shrimp and fish cake slices. Cook until the shrimp turn pink and the fish cake is heated through.
  5. In bowls, divide the cooked rice noodles, then pour the soup with shrimp and fish cake over the noodles.
  6. Top with bean sprouts and fresh herbs like cilantro or laksa leaves.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
